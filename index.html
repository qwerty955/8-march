Happy Woman's Day!

with the eighth of March

Beauty reigns supreme again.
Hear her breathing!
The best will become a pedestal
Sincere wishes:


Be young like spring
Fresh as the first snowdrop!
May it always be with you
A loving and faithful companion


May good luck not leave
The heart will light up with feeling.
You're beautiful! It means
Fortunately, I can't hide from you!
